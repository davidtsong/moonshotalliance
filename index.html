We are a global community of ‘crazy ones’ who are setting out to build a better future. Launched out of Stanford University, we’re collecting and connecting inventors, entrepreneurs, and students who share our goal.

Moonshot Academy @ Perth

For the first time ever, Moonshot is streaming live from Silicon Valley to ‘Satellite Academies’ across the globe. Blending the accessibility of online content with the support of in-person experiences, our live-streamed talks and classes will feature some of the best in Silicon Valley and Stanford University, teaching you how to ‘THINK BIG’ before your local coaches help you apply what you’ve learned to your world-changing idea. If you’re crazy enough to think you can change the world, apply to join us below and come invent the future.
